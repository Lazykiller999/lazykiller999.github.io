Things about me

Hi there! My name is Julio, and I'm an aspiring games developer. I have always had a passion for games since I was a child. Playing games has been an important part of my life and has helped shape me into who I am today. As a child, I loved immersing myself in different worlds and stories, and video games provided me with an escape from the real world. I was fascinated by how games could take me on exciting adventures, solve puzzles, and test my skills. What I didn't realize back then was that playing games was helping me develop important life skills. Games taught me to think creatively, work well under pressure, and approach challenges with a positive attitude. These skills have been invaluable to me in my personal and professional life. I bring that same passion and enthusiasm for problem-solving and creativity to my work. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope you will enjoy getting to know me and my work a little better.
